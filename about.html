My Story

My name is Nicholas Harrison. I was born in Plano, Texas, but I have lived in sunny Naples, Florida for nearly 15 years now. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering at Florida Gulf Coast University (FGCU).

When I graduate, I hope to work in the aerospace industry, particularly in astronautics. I have always loved space, and would love even more to work on a project that aids humankind in further exploring the vast expanse that is space.

In my spare time, I mostly play video games, read, or work on a project. History is one of my favorite subjects, second only to computer science, and I have loved it since I was very young. I think history is one of the most important topics anyone can study, as it gives us one of the most invaluable pieces of information that exists: context. I am thus particularly fond of historical literature and strategy games, or any game that immerses you fully in its environment (whether historical or otherwise). Aside from reading and playing video games, I also like to make great use of where I live by going to the beach as regularly as possible--nothing clears my head like being in nature.
